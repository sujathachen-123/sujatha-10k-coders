THE RAMESHWARAM CAFE

The Rameshwaram Cafe is popular because of their exquisite and authentic South Indian menu, quick service, extremely high-quality food served hot right away and their ability to make traditional recipes passed down for generations relevant to today’s on-the-go crowd.

Why is it called Rameshwaram cafe

Together, they launched The Rameshwaram Cafe; a name chosen to pay tribute to the late Mr. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam’s birthplace - “They have dedicated this brand to Dr APJ Abdul Kalam as Rameshwaram was his birthplace. Another logical reason is because we are into pure veg, authentic South Indian food and we want to go across the globe.
